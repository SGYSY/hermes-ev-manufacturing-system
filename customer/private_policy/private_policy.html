Privacy Policy

At Hermes, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data when you interact with our services:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Name, email address, phone number, and billing address.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, billing information, and transaction history.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, apps, and services, including IP addresses, device information, and browsing behavior.
  • Location Data: GPS data and location-related information if you use our services that involve location tracking (e.g., vehicle rentals).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Process transactions, bookings, and vehicle rentals or purchases.
  • To Improve Services: Analyze usage data to enhance our services, website functionality, and user experience.
  • To Communicate with You: Send notifications, confirmations, marketing materials, and important updates regarding your use of our services.
  • To Meet Legal Requirements: Comply with applicable laws and regulations, including data protection laws.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission (SSL/TLS protocols).
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel can access your data.

However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our services (e.g., payment processors, marketing partners).
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as to comply with legal processes, court orders, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Hermes, our customers, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal obligations and legitimate interests.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website and app. These technologies help us remember your preferences, analyze website traffic, and provide personalized content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make updates, we will post the revised policy on our website and notify you through the contact details we have on file. Continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
